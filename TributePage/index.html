"The Weeknd"

Abel Makkonen Tesfaye.

A Dark Past

"I did't have a father figure in the house, No boys around. Just me and my mom." "I could't ever go back home without being something. I probably would never have gone back home," he said. "That was definitely a big motivation. To get back home, and not empty-handed". At 17, he decided on a whim to move out of his mother's house and into a one-bedroom apartment with a friend. The pair partied and stole food from supermarkets, spending the money they had on alcohol and drugs.

Give back to the community

Someone with a dark past can become an empathetic person. After "making it", Abel has dedicated himself to helping those who need help.

  • Donation for war crisis in Ethiopia.
  • In association with Postmates, a food donation is made to people who work in the health sector.
  • 500,000 USD donation to BLM.
  • 300,000 USD donation to victims un Beirut port.
  • 1,000,000 USD donation to provide food to the Ethiopian citizens.
  • Abel becomes a ONU ambassador to provide food to those who need it.
